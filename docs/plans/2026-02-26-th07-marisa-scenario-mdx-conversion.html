Design Doc: TH07 Marisa Scenario MDX Conversion

Overview​

Convert docs/mainline/gen1/th07/dialogue/marisa-scenario.md to .mdx format to support interactive components (<Dialogue>, <Action>) and maintain consistency with other dialogue files (e.g., TH06).

Goals​

  • Precise conversion of Markdown table dialogue to MDX components.
  • Extraction of translator notes/comments into component props.
  • Standardization of BGM actions.
  • Functional ending links with correct anchors.

Design​

1. Frontmatter​

  • title: "마리사 시나리오"
  • sidebar_position: 5
  • description: "동방요요몽 마리사 루트 스토리를 스테이지별 대사 표로 정리했습니다."

2. Components​

  • Dialogue: <Dialogue speaker="..." comment="...">...</Dialogue>
    • Used for character lines.
    • comment prop contains text from parentheses or after .......
  • Action: <Action>...</Action>
    • Used for character entrances/exits, titles, and BGM.
    • BGM format: ♪ Title (links removed).

3. Link Mapping​

  • Update ending links to point to specific anchors in marisa-endings.mdx.
    • #2 -> #배드-엔딩-no-2-마리사
    • #6 -> #굿-엔딩-no-6-마리사-a
    • #7 -> #굿-엔딩-no-7-마리사-b

Verification Plan​

  • Manual Review: Ensure no text is lost during conversion.
  • LSP Diagnostics: Check for JSX syntax errors.
  • Docusaurus Build: (Optional) Verify that the MDX renders correctly.
